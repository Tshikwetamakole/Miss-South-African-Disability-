About Miss South Africa Disability

Empowering women with disabilities through advocacy, representation, and opportunity

Our Mission

The Miss South Africa Disability pageant is a national platform dedicated to celebrating the innate beauty, resilience, and powerful advocacy of women living with disabilities across South Africa. We aim to empower, inspire, and create opportunities for inclusive representation and change.

Our Vision

To be a leading force in promoting inclusivity, empowerment, and social change for women with disabilities in South Africa and beyond.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity
  • Empowerment
  • Advocacy
  • Respect
  • Integrity
