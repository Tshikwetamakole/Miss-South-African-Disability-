About Us

Miss South Africa Disability is a pioneering platform dedicated to empowering women with disabilities through inclusive beauty pageants, advocacy programs, and leadership development initiatives.

Our mission is to challenge stereotypes, break down barriers, and create a world where women with disabilities are celebrated for their abilities, beauty, and potential. We believe that true beauty transcends physical appearance and lies in the strength, resilience, and determination of the human spirit.

Through our programs, we aim to provide a platform for women with disabilities to showcase their talents, share their stories, and become leaders and role models in their communities. We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment where every participant feels valued, respected, and empowered.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We believe in creating a space where every woman, regardless of her disability, feels welcome, respected, and valued.
  • Empowerment: We are dedicated to providing women with the tools, skills, and confidence to become powerful advocates for themselves and their communities.
  • Authenticity: We celebrate the unique stories and identities of our participants, encouraging them to embrace their true selves.
  • Advocacy: We are committed to using our platform to challenge stereotypes and champion the rights and inclusion of all persons with disabilities.
