About Miss South Africa Disability

Miss South Africa Disability is more than a pageant; it's a movement. We are a pioneering platform dedicated to empowering women with disabilities through inclusive beauty pageants, advocacy programs, and leadership development initiatives.

Our mission is to challenge stereotypes, break down barriers, and create a world where women with disabilities are celebrated for their abilities, beauty, and potential.

Empowerment

Building confidence and leadership skills for a new generation of change-makers.

Advocacy

Championing disability rights and promoting a more inclusive and accessible society.

Community

Creating supportive networks and fostering a sense of belonging and sisterhood.
