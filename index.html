About Miss South Africa Disability

Miss South Africa Disability is a pioneering platform dedicated to empowering women with disabilities through inclusive beauty pageants, advocacy programs, and leadership development initiatives.

Our mission is to challenge stereotypes, break down barriers, and create a world where women with disabilities are celebrated for their abilities, beauty, and potential.
