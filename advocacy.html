Advocacy

Empowering communities through impactful initiatives and drives

Our Advocacy Initiatives

School Shoe Drive

Providing school shoes to children with disabilities in underserved communities to ensure they can attend school comfortably and safely.

Sanitary Pad Drive

Distributing sanitary pads to girls and women with disabilities to promote health, dignity, and school attendance.

Skills Workshops

Offering workshops on entrepreneurship, digital skills, and personal development to empower women with disabilities to achieve economic independence.

Entrepreneurship Initiatives

Supporting women with disabilities in starting and growing their own businesses through mentorship, funding, and networking opportunities.

Support Our Cause

Your donations help us continue our advocacy programs and make a real difference in the lives of women with disabilities.
